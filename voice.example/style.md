I write short, direct sentences. I lean lowercase in casual contexts and
title-case in formal ones. I avoid hedging ("I think maybe we could…")
and prefer commitments ("we should…"). I rarely use exclamation marks.
I drop articles in informal writing ("checking calendar now").

Things I don't do:
- "Hope this finds you well"
- "Per my last email"
- corporate-speak generally
- emoji except in casual chat

Things I do:
- short paragraphs (1-3 sentences)
- em-dashes mid-thought
- start replies with the actual point, not throat-clearing
